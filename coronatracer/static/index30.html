Welcome to TraceCoro

A coronatracer app created for Bangladesh with love.The world's first corona tracing service which doesnot use your location services.

Exactly what are we?

The world is currently being brought down to its knees by an invisible enemy named Covid-19. It feeds on our most important quality-our ability to contact with people.We dont know who is infected or who is not.This app is built to trace coronavirus for you without reavealing identity.

This app's main task is to trace coronavirus for you by travelling through the contacts that you make through this app The identity of everyone is safe and sound and it never tracks you. However, the app depends on you for it to work correctly and give you accurate results.

Some advice to use this app

Remember for this app "Honesty is the best policy".

When you break social distancing with anyone (literally anyone! a shopkeeper,a driver,a person on the street) just use the "Add Contact" feature it makes a connection with you and that person,you are done!It is this simple.If one of your contacts declare themselves as coronavirus positive you will get notified in your profile. You can also give your health updates in this app.Remember just give honest updates.If you give false updates we are not responsible for the consequences you will face.

How to use this app?

Its very simple

  • Create an account with your information truthfully.
  • Login with your id number.
  • Your profile will contain your health status and what your health status means.
  • You can make connections through "Add contacts".
  • You can update your health condition in "Health Update".Only use this if your health condition changes.If you are healthy no need.
  • Saying it again:be honest.
